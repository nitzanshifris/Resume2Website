# Template v0_template_v1.5 Specification v1.0

## Template Identity
**Name**: Professional Standard  
**Target**: Universal professional appeal across all industries  
**Design Philosophy**: Clean, trustworthy, and immediately credible

## Intent
Provide a universally professional portfolio template that works equally well for a software engineer, nurse, accountant, teacher, or sales representative. Focus on clarity, credibility, and ease of information consumption.

## Target User Success Stories

### Software Developer
*"I need to showcase my technical skills and projects in a way that doesn't overwhelm non-technical recruiters, but still impresses technical hiring managers."*

### Healthcare Professional  
*"I want to highlight my certifications and patient care experience in a professional way that healthcare administrators will respect."*

### Sales Representative
*"I need to show my achievement numbers and client success stories in a format that demonstrates my results-driven approach."*

### Career Changer
*"I'm transitioning industries and need a template that makes my transferable skills obvious while looking professional in my new field."*

## Design Values & Principles

### 1. **Universal Professionalism**
- Conservative enough for traditional industries (finance, healthcare, government)
- Modern enough for contemporary companies (tech, startups, creative agencies)
- Appropriate for all experience levels (entry to executive)

### 2. **Information Clarity**
- Clear visual hierarchy guides the eye to most important information
- Consistent typography creates professional reading experience
- White space prevents overwhelming busy recruiters

### 3. **Trust & Credibility**
- Design choices build immediate confidence in the candidate
- Nothing flashy or gimmicky that might detract from content
- Professional polish that reflects attention to detail

### 4. **Accessibility First**
- High contrast for easy reading
- Clear fonts that work on all devices
- Logical information flow for screen readers

## Content Strategy

### Information Hierarchy (What Users See First)
1. **Hero Section**: Name, title, professional photo, contact info
2. **Professional Summary**: 2-3 line career overview with key achievements
3. **Core Skills**: Industry-relevant skills prominently displayed
4. **Professional Experience**: Detailed work history with quantified achievements
5. **Education & Certifications**: Academic and professional credentials
6. **Projects/Portfolio**: Relevant work samples (when applicable)
7. **Additional Sections**: Based on individual CV content

### Achievement-Focused Presentation
- Every role shows measurable impact where possible
- Skills linked to real-world applications
- Projects demonstrate problem-solving capabilities
- Education connects to career goals
